# Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
Create visualizations that convey a compelling business story using Power BI.

## Dataset
Retail sales dataset containing customer, product, region, and sales information.

## Visualizations Created

1. KPI Cards
   - Total Sales
   - Total Orders
   - Total Customers

2. Sales Trend Analysis
   - Sales by Year and Category

3. Customer Segment Analysis
   - Revenue contribution by segment

4. Regional Analysis
   - Sales by region

5. Product Category Analysis
   - Average sales by category

## Key Insights

- Consumer segment generates the highest revenue.
- Technology products have the highest average sales value.
- West and East regions contribute the majority of revenue.
- Sales show an upward trend over time.

## Recommendations

- Increase marketing investment toward Consumer customers.
- Expand Technology product offerings.
- Investigate low sales performance in the South region.
- Replicate successful strategies from high-performing regions.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
